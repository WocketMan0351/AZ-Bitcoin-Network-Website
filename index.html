Topics

 
Protocol

We enjoy discussing bitcoin from a technical and computer science perspective to help everyone better understand what makes it work in a secure, robust, and decentralized way.

 
Economics

Could bitcoin be the world's new money? What are the implications of separating money from state, and how does bitcoin stack up against other forms of money?

 
Software

Mobile applications, companies, and many services are being built around bitcoin. By coming together and sharing new things, we help each other stay at the forefront of innovation in this rapidly expanding space.

 
Investing

We encourage taking a long-term view when investing in bitcoin. A strategy we commonly recommend is Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) into bitcoin regardless of price. We're here for the long run. HODL!

 
Storage

The #1 rule of BTC is "Not your keys, not your coin!" Bitcoin can seem intimidating at first, but we love teaching newcomers how to keep their bitcoin safe. We can assure you it's easier than you probably think!

 
Lightning

The Lightning network is a higher layer solution that is currently scaling bitcoin in a way that avoids compromising the decentralization at the base layer — the blockchain. Come out and make your first Lightning network transaction for free!

About Us

We're a group focused on all things Bitcoin. All experience levels welcome, whether you're new and curious or have been in the scene for years. We meet regularly for casual discussions and presentations on topics like the protocol and software, economics, trading, storage techniques, investing, and mining. Come out to meet, learn from, and share ideas with fellow Bitcoiners!
